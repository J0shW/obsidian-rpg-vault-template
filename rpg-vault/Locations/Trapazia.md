## Landmarks
### Suclidian Ocean
The primary body of water that falls to the west of Trapazia. Not much is known past this body of water as any explorers who have ventured to discover new lands have never returned.

### The Central Planes
The vast and beautiful green meadows that surround [[Adelgate]]. While beautiful and vast, many dangerous creatures call this place home.
